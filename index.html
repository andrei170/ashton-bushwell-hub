Compliance Notes

+

The build keeps the brand carefully on the educator side of sales coaching. Everything we ship for you has these guardrails:

  • NO income guarantees, earnings projections, or "you will close X deals" promises anywhere in copy
  • Big career numbers (250K-plus doors, 100K-plus trained, 22 deals in a day, 1,000-plus installs) all flagged as self-reported, the source carries the claim
  • Coachability disclaimer on the application page and in the final nurture email (results depend on the rep's effort, vertical, and market)
  • Faith and family framed as values and integrity, not religion (no LDS or bishop language in copy)
  • NO fabricated student testimonials or named student wins (none were extractable from public sources)
  • Conservative training number used in copy ("1,000-plus salespeople trained") even where higher numbers appear in your bios

This keeps you defensible while still letting you sell the mentorship and the method confidently.
